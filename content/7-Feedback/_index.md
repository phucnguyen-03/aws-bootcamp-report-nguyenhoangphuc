> This section presents my personal reflections and suggestions after completing the internship program at AWS First Cloud AI Journey.

# General Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment was professional, friendly, and highly collaborative. Throughout the internship, mentors and other participants were always willing to share their knowledge and provide support whenever difficulties arose. The learning atmosphere encouraged curiosity, independent thinking, and continuous improvement. I especially appreciated the opportunity to work on real cloud technologies rather than only learning theoretical concepts.

### 2. Mentor and Administrator Support

The mentors were knowledgeable, approachable, and supportive throughout the internship. Instead of simply providing answers, they encouraged interns to analyze problems independently and guided us toward appropriate solutions. Regular feedback sessions helped us improve both our technical skills and our problem-solving abilities. The program administrators also provided timely support regarding schedules, learning materials, and internship activities.

### 3. Relevance to My Academic Background

The internship aligned well with my Software Engineering major while introducing many new technologies that were not covered in university courses. Working with AWS Cloud services, Serverless Architecture, and Generative AI allowed me to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world software development practices.

### 4. Learning and Skill Development

The internship provided valuable opportunities to strengthen both technical and soft skills. Besides learning AWS services such as Amazon EC2, Amazon S3, Amazon VPC, AWS Lambda, Amazon DynamoDB, Amazon Cognito, and Amazon Bedrock, I also improved my abilities in:

- Cloud architecture design
- System analysis and problem solving
- Team collaboration
- Project planning and task management
- Technical documentation
- Presentation and communication skills

Developing the **AI-Powered Smart Document Assistant** project allowed me to apply these skills in a practical development environment.

### 5. Company Culture and Teamwork

The program promoted a positive and collaborative culture where everyone respected each other's ideas and was willing to help whenever necessary. Team activities, technical discussions, and knowledge-sharing sessions created an excellent environment for learning. Even as an intern, I felt that my ideas and contributions were appreciated by mentors and teammates.

### 6. Internship Program Benefits

One of the greatest advantages of this internship was the structured learning roadmap. The program combined technical training, hands-on laboratories, project development, knowledge-sharing sessions, and AWS-related competitions. These activities helped interns progressively build practical cloud computing skills while preparing for real industry projects.

# Additional Questions

### What was the most satisfying part of your internship?

The most rewarding experience was having the opportunity to build a complete cloud application using AWS services. Developing the **AI-Powered Smart Document Assistant** project helped me understand how multiple AWS services work together in a real-world architecture. I also enjoyed participating in technical sharing sessions and AWS knowledge competitions, which made the learning experience both engaging and practical.

### What improvements would you suggest for future internship programs?

The internship program was well organized overall. One possible improvement would be to provide additional hands-on workshops focusing on advanced AWS services and real production scenarios. More collaborative coding sessions and architecture review activities could also help interns gain deeper practical experience.

### Would you recommend this internship to your friends? Why?

Yes, definitely.

I would highly recommend this internship to students who are interested in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence. The program offers a comprehensive learning path, experienced mentors, practical projects, and opportunities to work with modern AWS technologies. It provides valuable experience that prepares students for future careers in cloud engineering and software development.

- Organize additional hands-on workshops for advanced AWS services.

### Would you like to continue participating in this program?

Yes.

Given the opportunity, I would be very interested in continuing to participate in future AWS learning programs. I hope to further develop my cloud computing knowledge, gain more practical experience, and contribute to larger projects involving AWS and Artificial Intelligence technologies.

### Additional Comments

Overall, this internship was an extremely valuable learning experience. It not only strengthened my technical knowledge of AWS Cloud but also helped me develop professional working habits, improve communication and teamwork skills, and gain a clearer understanding of software development in a real business environment. I sincerely appreciate the support provided by the mentors, administrators, and everyone involved in making this internship both meaningful and inspiring.
